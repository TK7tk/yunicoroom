# Extraction 03: Business Impact & ROI of Autonomous Agents 2025

## Market Size & Growth
-   **2025 Market**: Estimated ~$7.63 Billion to $10.4 Billion.
-   **Future Projection**: Expected to reach ~$200 - $477 Billion by 2035 (CAGR 40-56%).
-   **Economic Contribution**: Generative AI (including agents) to contribute $2.6-$4.4 Trillion annually to global GDP.

## ROI & Efficiency
-   **ROI**: 74% of executives see ROI within the first year. 88% of early adopters report positive ROI.
-   **Efficiency Gains**: ~30% increase in operational efficiency.
-   **Cost Reduction**: Finance-related costs expected to drop by 40%.
-   **Case Studies**:
    -   Walmart: +12% profit margins via pricing AI.
    -   PayPal: Blocks $6B fraud annually.

## Workforce Transformation
-   **Autonomous Decisions**: By 2028, 15% of work decisions will be made by Agentic AI (up from 0% in 2024).
-   **Adoption Rate**: 25% of GenAI companies will pilot agents in 2025, rising to 50% by 2027.
-   **Shift**: Humans moving to strategic/creative tasks; Agents handling 24/7 operations.
